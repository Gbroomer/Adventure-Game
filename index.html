Characters can have anywhere between 8 and 15 for their starting ability scores.
Ability scores have a modifier attached to them--the modifiers are set at 2 point intervals.
An 8 results in a -1 ability modifier, a 10 results in a 0, a 12 results in a +1, and so on.
Ability scores are purchased using the DnD 5e point-buy system.
